What We Do

01
Full Auto Repairs

All makes and models of private passenger and light commercial vehicles. From minor fixes to major overhauls — we handle it all under one roof.

02
Safety Inspections

Official Ontario MTO safety inspections performed on-site. Get your vehicle certified quickly and confidently by our qualified technicians.

03
Wheel Alignments

Precision wheel alignments to extend tire life and improve handling. We set your vehicle straight so you drive straight — every kilometre.

04
Brake Service

Full brake inspections, pad replacement, rotor resurfacing, and caliper service. We make sure you can stop safely — every single time.

05
Engine Diagnostics

Modern diagnostic equipment to accurately pinpoint issues. We find the real problem — not just the symptom — and fix it right the first time.

06
Loaner Vehicles

Need to keep moving while your car is in the shop? Loaner vehicles are available upon request — just mention it when you call to book.
